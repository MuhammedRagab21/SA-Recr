# Lekker Abroad

## Business Summary

We are building Lekker Abroad: a South African-focused relocation, training, and English-teaching placement support agency for people who want to move to Northern Vietnam.

The business model is inspired by Freedom Vietnam's category and funnel, but the brand, copy, proof, package details, and positioning must be our own. We are copying the working business architecture, not creating a one-to-one public knock-off.

Core promise:

> Helping South Africans teach, earn, and save in Northern Vietnam.

Working brand name:

> Lekker Abroad

Current visual direction:

> Oxblood Passport: clean near-white paper, oxblood/black ink, brass salary accents, and route blue used sparingly for Diamond/priority moments. Vietnam photography remains essential; the palette should not lean on obvious Vietnam green or generic money lime.

Longer positioning:

> We help South Africans relocate to Northern Vietnam, prepare for English teaching jobs, secure opportunities, and settle in with step-by-step support before and after arrival.

The business should lead with money-driven outcomes:

- Earn more than many entry-level or mid-level options in South Africa.
- Spend less because Northern Vietnam can offer a lower cost of living.
- Save faster through better income-to-expense potential.
- Start a new life with a guided path instead of figuring everything out alone.

## Differentiation

The brand should be close enough to attract the same audience as Freedom Vietnam, but distinct enough to stand on its own.

Primary difference:

- Northern Vietnam specialization.

Support differences:

- Stronger focus on earning, saving, and financial progress.
- More detailed training and preparation materials.
- More hands-on relocation and arrival support.
- South African community and familiar guidance.
- Clearer package details and bonus resources.
- Accessible entry through pricing set below Freedom Vietnam's visible prices.

Internal positioning:

> Same destination category, same South African audience, same English-teaching outcome, but focused on Northern Vietnam, stronger financial framing, more detailed support, and more transparent offer structure.

## Audience

Primary audience:

> South Africans who want to leave South Africa, earn better money, reduce living costs, and build savings through English teaching in Northern Vietnam.

Best-fit candidate:

- South African.
- Serious about relocating to Northern Vietnam.
- Money-motivated and wants to earn/save.
- Has enough money to fund relocation, documents, and service fees.
- Can communicate professionally in English.
- Willing to complete training and follow the process.
- Open to teaching children, teenagers, or adults.
- Willing to prepare documents properly.
- Open to job opportunities across Northern Vietnam.

Secondary audience:

- People without a degree.
- People without TEFL/TESOL yet.
- People without teaching experience.
- Career changers.
- People who need more confidence or interview preparation.
- People who need extra document guidance.

Important public wording:

> We assess each candidate's eligibility and recommend the best available route based on their documents, qualifications, timeline, and goals.

Do not publicly promise that every underqualified person can be placed.

Reject early:

- People with no money or no realistic way to fund the move.
- People expecting free service.
- People who refuse training or interview preparation.
- People who want to work illegally or cut visa/work-permit corners.
- People with serious document, legal, criminal, or health-check risks.
- People who cannot communicate professionally in English.
- People who are not willing to relocate to Northern Vietnam.
- People who expect results without effort.

Audience one-liner:

> For South Africans who are ready to stop waiting, move abroad, teach English, and save properly.

## Offer

We sell a paid relocation, training, job-preparation, and English-teaching placement support service for South Africans moving to Northern Vietnam.

We are not only selling job applications. We are selling a guided pathway:

- Prepare the candidate.
- Improve their teaching readiness.
- Organize documents.
- Build their CV/profile.
- Help them apply to schools and centers.
- Prepare them for interviews and demo lessons.
- Help them compare offers.
- Guide relocation and first steps after arrival.

Offer statement:

> We offer three guided relocation and English-teaching support packages for South Africans moving to Northern Vietnam. Each package helps candidates prepare, apply, interview, relocate, and settle in with the right level of support for their budget and confidence level.

## Pricing Model

Pricing is set at 75% of Freedom Vietnam's visible package prices.

Use one official currency in final sales material, with approximate conversions only where needed because exchange rates move.

Current draft pricing:

| Package | Upfront Fee | Later Payment |
| --- | ---: | ---: |
| Starter | 450 USD / approx. R7,500 | 135 million VND over 12 months |
| Premium | 1,125 USD / approx. R18,750 | 180 million VND over 12 months |
| Diamond | 4,500 USD / approx. R75,000 | 360 million VND over 24 months |

Draft later-payment rule:

> Later payments begin only after the candidate receives their first salary in Vietnam.

This must eventually be supported by clear written terms covering:

- Payment start date.
- Missed payments.
- Candidate quitting.
- Candidate changing schools.
- Candidate refusing reasonable offers.
- Candidate disappearing.
- Candidate failing eligibility requirements.
- Refund policy.

## Guarantee Model

The business will use strong public guarantees, but the internal rules must stay precise.

Guarantee remedy:

> If the candidate completes the process, meets the requirements, and does not receive a qualifying offer, we continue supporting them until they do.

There is no refund guarantee for now.

Starter guarantee:

- No job placement guarantee.
- No salary guarantee.

Premium public guarantee:

> Job Placement Guaranteed: complete the process, meet the requirements, and we'll help you secure a teaching job in Northern Vietnam.

Premium internal meaning:

> If the candidate completes all required steps and meets eligibility requirements, support continues until they receive a qualifying teaching job offer.

Diamond public guarantee:

> Job + Salary Guaranteed: complete the process, meet the requirements, and we'll help you secure a Northern Vietnam teaching role at or above our Diamond salary benchmark.

Diamond internal meaning:

> If the candidate completes all required steps and meets eligibility requirements, support continues until they receive a qualifying teaching job offer that meets or exceeds the Diamond salary benchmark.

Terms still need to define:

- Complete the process.
- Meet the requirements.
- Qualifying offer.
- Diamond salary benchmark.
- Candidate disqualification rules.
- Refusal of a reasonable offer.

## Package 1: Starter

Purpose:

> For people who want access to the roadmap, training, tools, and application resources, but are willing to do more of the work themselves.

Includes:

- Candidate readiness assessment.
- Northern Vietnam relocation roadmap.
- Full document checklist (visas, work permits, police clearances).
- Visa/work-permit guidance.
- Access to training videos (7+ modules including ESL methods, interview mastery, class games).
- Interview preparation material.
- Demo lesson training material.
- CV rebuild or CV improvement support.
- Application email templates.
- Access to 700+ school/center contact database.
- School application tracker.
- Northern Vietnam education guide.
- Cost-of-living and savings calculator.
- First 30 Days in Vietnam Playbook.
- Private community access.
- 2 x 30-minute consultation calls.
- 3 months text/WhatsApp support.
- Airport pickup coordination & check-in support.

Does not include:

- Job placement guarantee.
- Salary guarantee.
- Done-for-you applications.
- Unlimited calls.
- Priority support.
- Deep one-on-one coaching.

Public positioning:

> Best for independent candidates who want the tools, training, and contacts to start their Northern Vietnam teaching journey.

Includes everything in Starter, plus:

- Personalized relocation timeline.

- Done-For-You professional teacher profile setup.
- Done-For-You cover letter / school intro message setup.
- Guided interview coaching & practice interview sessions.
- Guided demo lesson coaching & custom lesson plan feedback.
- Done-For-You school application strategy & scheduling.
- Salary and contract negotiation support.
- Done-For-You interview scheduling with verified schools.
- On-the-ground accommodation search assistance and lease review.
- Pre-arrival orientation call.
- First-week setup guidance (SIM cards, bank account, motorbike rentals).
- 5 x 30-minute consultation calls.
- 3 months WhatsApp/text support.
- Job placement guarantee.

Public guarantee:

> Job Placement Guaranteed: complete the process, meet the requirements, and we'll help you secure a teaching job in Northern Vietnam.

This should be the main offer pushed in content and sales calls.

## Package 3: Diamond

Purpose:

> For people who want the highest level of support, priority handling, and stronger income outcome.

Includes everything in Premium, plus:

- 3 months fully-paid premium local accommodation in Hanoi/Northern Vietnam.
- Daily meals & local transport stipend covered for the first 3 months.
- VIP Airport concierge meet-and-greet on arrival.
- Priority support & extended support period.
- 6 months WhatsApp/text support.
- Unlimited consultation calls for the first 3 months, within fair-use rules.
- More intensive 1-on-1 interview and demo lesson coaching.
- Advanced classroom confidence training.
- More active school application support.
- Priority application tracking.
- More hands-on document guidance.
- 6 months monthly check-ins and career growth guidance after placement.
- Personal savings plan and job offer decision scorecard.
- Job + salary guarantee (minimum salary lock).

Public guarantee:

> Job + Salary Guaranteed: complete the process, meet the requirements, and we'll help you secure a Northern Vietnam teaching role at or above our Diamond salary benchmark.



## 700+ School/Center Contact Database

The business has access to a file/text list of 700+ emails for schools, centers, and education-related places in Vietnam.

Position this carefully:

> Access to our school and language-center application database, including 700+ school/center contacts across Vietnam, plus application templates and tracking support.

Do not position it as:

- 700 guaranteed opportunities.
- Guaranteed responses.
- Guaranteed interviews.
- Guaranteed jobs.

The database should support:

- Self-led applications in Starter.
- Guided applications in Premium.
- Priority/active support in Diamond.

## Important Risk Notes

The strongest risks are:

- Overpromising job or salary outcomes.
- Accepting candidates who are not legally or practically placeable.
- Making public claims about underqualified candidates that cannot be supported.
- Using copied brand assets, text, testimonials, or screenshots from another business.
- Selling the 700+ email list as guaranteed jobs rather than an application resource.

The public brand can use strong guarantees, but the contract and operating process must define eligibility, candidate responsibilities, qualifying offers, and disqualification rules clearly.
