##  We help people move to Vietnam, and start a better life. But what's so great about living in Vietnam? 

##  More money - English teachers in Vietnam earn from 1500 to 5000 USD. (25 000 to 90 000 ZAR). There are unlimited job opportunities and you have complete financial freedom. 

##  Less expenses - most things are much cheaper in Vietnam. This includes food, clothes, fun activities, and even transport. Therefore, it becomes much easier to save massive amounts of money every single month. 

##  Social life - You will never get bored in Ho Chi Minh City! The people are always kind and friendly. There are so many social clubs, events, communities, and the night life is amazing. It's a place filled with vibrance, culture and friendly people. 

##  Saftey - Vietnam's crime rates are almost 20 times lower than South Africa. Serious crimes are almost completely non-existent. This means that you can you walk around late at night and go wherever you want without ever needing to worry about your safety. 

##  Food - Most people will agree that Vietnamese cuisine is one of the best in the world. Some Vietnamese favourites are Pho noodle soup, Vietnamese spring rolls and Banh mi sandwiches. There is also a massive variety of international cuisine such as Mexican, Japanese, Italian, Korean and even South African restaurants. 

##  Tourism - Vietnam is famous for it's absolutely magnificent scenery, natural landscapes, cultural heritage sights and many other tourist attractions. Traveling in Vietnam is also much cheaper than most other places in the world. Hotels, food, transport and even tours are much cheaper. This means you can travel more often! 

##  Weather - If you're a person who loves summer, then Ho Chi Minh City is the place to be. The tropical climate is characterized by warm temperatures throughout the entire year. Especially if you like swimming, going to the beach, water sports and pool parties! 

##  FREEDOM - This is by far the best thing about living in Vietnam. Having more money, more time, more experiences, and more adventures. Living in Vietnam will completely change your entire outlook on life - you will become a completely different person.
