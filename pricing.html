Information Desk

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is there an upfront administrative fee?

expand_more
The upfront administrative fee covers essential hard costs incurred before you arrive in Vietnam. This includes state-accredited TEFL course registration, done-for-you CV alignment, and administrative hours spent by our ground team vetting positions and routing templates.

When do the post-placement installments begin?

expand_more
Installments only begin once you are successfully placed in a school and have received your first full month's salary in Vietnam. Payments are paid in local VND or equivalent over a 12-month period (24 months for the Diamond package), allowing you to pay for your relocation out of your earnings.

What happens if I don't get placed in a school?

expand_more
For our Premium and Diamond packages, we offer a strict Job Placement Guarantee. If you meet the basic legal requirements (clean background check, valid bachelor's degree) and complete our training modules but do not secure a contract offer, we will refund your upfront administrative fee in full.

Are flights and visa processing fees included in the upfront cost?

expand_more
No, upfront packages do not include flight tickets or direct government visa/legalization fees. These are personal costs. However, our ground team provides full administrative templates, DIRCO legalizing guidelines, and school sponsorship document routing to ensure you do not make costly administrative mistakes.
